ABOUT ME

Just a gal from Houston, TX who found her place in North Central Washington.

I love music, immersive art, motorcycles, travel, pit bulls, hiking, running, baking, and creating! After over 10 years in hospitality, I moved into advertising and marketing and began my MS in Art and Technology from the University of Oklahoma. Throughout the program, I explored numerous digital art mediums and landed on wanting to learn different coding languages to use for AR and VR design, as well as point me toward the path of front end development. I've most enjoyed using Unity for creating 3D virtual environments, Glitch.com to create VR spaces and working in Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects to create videos. Still working out the rest!

Please enjoy some of my favorite pieces created through various Adobe programs, Unity, and Glitch. More projects created with various coding languages are soon to come!
